2
The Story
Read carefully. Notice the bold words.
🐎

The Mycenaeans were also the first Greeks to use horses in battle. Before this, soldiers had always fought on foot, marching and fighting shoulder to shoulder.

But the Mycenaeans began to hitch horses to war-chariots. They drove these two-wheeled chariots straight into the heart of battle, faster than any soldier could run.

Enemy soldiers terrified ran when they saw the warhorses and chariots charging straight at them! No one on foot had ever faced anything so fast, so loud, or so frightening.

With their armor, bronze weapons, and chariots working together, the Mycenaeans dominated the Aegean Sea and every island in it.

They were the first great Greek civilization — masters of the sea, masters of bronze, and now masters of the battlefield. But history has a way of humbling even the mightiest. The Mycenaeans didn't yet know it, but their golden age would not last forever.

Language Focus
Grammar from the story — learn through meaning
📌 Past Simple vs. Past Perfect
Use past perfect for a long-standing past situation, and past simple for the new event that changed it.
Before this, soldiers had always fought on foot.
But the Mycenaeans began to hitch horses to chariots.
Enemy soldiers had never seen chariots before — then they saw them charging.
💡 Past perfect = the old situation; past simple = what changed it.
📌 Verb + '-ing' after verbs of perception
After verbs like see, hear, and watch, we often use verb + '-ing' to describe an action in progress.
Enemy soldiers ran when they saw the chariots charging.
They watched the horses galloping toward them.
Soldiers feared seeing warhorses approaching.
💡 The '-ing' form shows the action was happening at that exact moment.
📌 Superlatives — 'the first', 'the strongest'
Use superlatives to show something is the most extreme example of its kind.
They were the first great Greek civilization.
The Mycenaeans became the strongest fighters in the Aegean.
Chariots were the newest weapon of that time.
💡 Superlatives usually add '-est' or use 'the most' for longer words.

Ideas & Society
Think about big ideas. There is no wrong answer.
🐎
The chariot was a brand-new invention that changed the way people fought. Can you think of other inventions in history that completely changed how wars were fought?
😱
Enemy soldiers fled simply from fear of the charging chariots. Do you think fear itself can be as powerful a weapon as actual force?
🏆
The story calls the Mycenaeans 'the first great Greek civilization.' What do you think makes a civilization 'great' — military power, culture, or something else?
🔄
Being first to use a new technology gave the Mycenaeans only a temporary advantage. Do you think being first with new technology always guarantees long-term success? Why or why not?
